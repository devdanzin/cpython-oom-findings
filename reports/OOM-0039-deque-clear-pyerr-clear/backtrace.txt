## Mechanism confirmation (window size k of set_nomemory(start, start+k), 2000-iter sweep)
# k=0 (fail-forever): FATAL    -- failure persists, newblock() in deque_clear fails -> PyErr_Clear
# k=1               : SURVIVES -- window closes before teardown; newblock() succeeds; no PyErr_Clear
# k=2 / k=4 / k=8   : FATAL    -- failure persists into the dealloc
