OOM-0035 — StringIO.getvalue() builds a str with an invalid maxchar from a buffer
left uninitialized by a write-under-OOM
==================================================================================

ft_debug_asan, SIGABRT. The StringIO's internal Py_UCS4 buffer (grown over many
writes under intermittent OOM) holds an uninitialized value > 0x10ffff inside
[0, string_size); getvalue() scans it, derives a bad maxchar, and the new str fails
its consistency check.

buffer; _PyUnicode_CheckConsistency:673 is the generic debug detector (reached by many
unicode ops), kept only for in-loop stdout matching.

(debug consistency assert; on release the malformed str survives, sometimes segvs on jit).
