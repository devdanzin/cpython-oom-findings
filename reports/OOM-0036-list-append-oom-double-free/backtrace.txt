and a later Py_XDECREF(entry->name/path) (during teardown of the entries list / its holding

Sequence under OOM:
  1. LOAD_ATTR_SLOT pushes an owned ref to e.a            (e.a refcount = 2: E.a slot + arg slot)
  2. _CALL_LIST_APPEND steals arg, hands it to AppendTakeRef
  3. list_resize fails (OOM) -> Py_DECREF(newitem)        (e.a refcount 2 -> 1; arg's ref consumed)
  4. ERROR_NO_POP() leaves the dead arg stackref on the value stack
  5. exception_unwind PyStackRef_XCLOSE(arg)              (e.a refcount 1 -> 0 -> FREED)
  6. E.a slot now dangles; clear_slots/DirEntry_dealloc Py_XDECREFs it -> negrefcount / SIGSEGV

Control: loading e.a WITHOUT the list.append does not crash -> the append is essential.
